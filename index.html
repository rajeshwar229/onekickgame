Help Modal

The goal of the game is to start kicking the nearest enemy. Enemies come from left & right side. Whoever is the nearest enemy to the hero, you should press that side arrow key. You will only have 1 to 3 seconds before the enemy kicks you, then the game is over. Your score is the number of enemies you kicked. Good luck for you and enjoy the game!
